About

Evil Eye Coffee has been making happy coffee since June of 2019. The combination of craft roasted coffee and house-made tea blends in a creative space makes stepping into Evil Eye Coffee a memorable and magical experience. Lara Miller's goal has always been to create inspiring spaces, with great people and amazing products, in a way that truly adds value to the community around us.
